ABOUT THE TOURNAMENT

All the Information You Need

Where?

The event will take place at Princeton Forrestal Center(off of Schalks Crossing Road and Research way) and Plainsboro Park(near Wyckoff Elementary)!

When?

The event is on September 28 and September 29, from 8AM - 7PM each day! The rain dates will be October 5 and October 6, from 8AM - 7PM.

Why Should I Go?

The tournament is sure to be a blast, with many teams competing in interesting matchups! Drop by for a great time!

Both parks will have lush scenery and a beautiful view of the cricket fields! Both parks will also have fun activities that you can participate in while you're waiting for the tournament. For example, near the building in the Princeton Forrestal Center contains research projects that Princeton University is working on, which you can explore!

Near the Plainsboro Park is the Lenape trail, which you can hike on with your family to pass some time. The Lenape trail is a unique and historic trail that allows you to trek several miles while viewing historic landmarks. It also offers a unique experience to hikers, as it is a relief from the eyesores caused by urbanizationg, allowing hikers to view some much-needed nature!

So when you come down to the cricket tournament, you are able to experience several other unique and fun experiences alongside watching interesting matches! Come down to enjoy yourself, while also getting away from technology for a fun filled evening!
