About Pearle

Pearle is now a Freshman at Drexel. She founded WWP NextGen because of many personal experiences that she had encountered in the past, such as stress after moving and her love for helping out her community. In an attempt to strengthen our younger generation, WWP NextGen was created to help solve problems that many of our children face today and help them gain the confidence they need to become stronger individuals.
